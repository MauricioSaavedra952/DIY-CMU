Problem Set 2: The ChipsBoxBoat

Description:

The second project involved conceptualizing a takeout box that would convert into another usable object. The ChipsBoxBoat transforms from a box to boat in 10 easy steps; and can easily store as a flat square, made entirely from recyclable materials.

 

Product Features:

  • Transforms from flat square to box to boat and back
  • 10 step transformation
  • Made entirely from recyclable materials
